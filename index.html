About Me

I started my career working in the trades — building, fixing, and figuring things out the hard way. Eventually, I picked up programming and now I create tools to help homeowners plan better, save money, and avoid getting ripped off by shady contractors. My mission is to bridge the gap between hands-on experience and modern tech, putting power back in the hands of regular people.
